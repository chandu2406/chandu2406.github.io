Welcome

Thanks for visiting.

I am a Computer Science graduate from NITK Surathkal, India. I am passionate about Technology and Education. My primary interest is in designing and studying innovative learning environments for modern learners, especially incorporating 21st Century Skills. Currently, I teach Math and Science to 9th graders at Shriram Vidya Mandir, a low income school in the suburbs of Mumabi, India. I work on several projects as part of Ubiquitous Learning Research Lab, a research group I founded to explore learning in the modern age.
