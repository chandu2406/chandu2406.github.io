Chandramouli Sharma

I am a school teacher at Sriram Vidya Mandir High School, a low-income K-10 school located in a chawl of Mumbai, India where I teach Maths, Science and Geography to 8th graders. I write about my teaching experiences on my teaching blog.

I am passionate about designing tools and platforms for making 21st century learning experiences joyful, purposeful, relevant and learner-driven. Here is an updated list of all my projects.

I have a keen interest in learning how learning happens in human beings. This research forms the foundations and guides all of my projects. My research interests include smart learning environments, educational data mining, visualization tools, Information architectures, and socio-economic factors in learning. I run a small research group called Ubiquitous Learning Research Lab at my Alma Mater, that deals in research in these areas.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from NITK Surathkal, India.
